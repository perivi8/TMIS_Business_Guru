Frequently Asked Questions How do I register a new user?

To register a new user, go to the registration page and use an email that starts with "tmis." or use "admin@gmail.com" for admin access. Fill in all required fields and submit the form.

 What documents are required for client registration?

Required documents include GST Document, Bank Statement (PDF), and MSME/Udyam Document. Additional documents may be required based on business type (Partnership, Proprietorship, or Private Limited).

 How do I update client status?

Only admin users can update client status. Go to the Clients page, click on the action menu for a client, and select the appropriate status (Interested, Not Interested, Hold, or Pending).

 Can I download client documents?

Yes, you can download client documents from the Clients page. Click on the action menu for a client and select the document you want to download from the Documents section.
